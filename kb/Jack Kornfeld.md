## Während des Vietnamkriegs in Laos:
“It is so, and you've all heard those stories many times, it is so because we are not separate. So that when I lived at [[Ajahn Chah]]'s monastery, in the province of [[Ubon]], beautiful old forest, great big tropical forest trees, we were not far from the border of [[Laos]] and [Cambodia](Kambotscha). You'd watch the bombers go overhead from the big air bases in the [Vietnam War](Vietnamkrieg), and then at night, you'd see the flashes of the bombs in Laos and Cambodia.”
