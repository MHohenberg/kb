In 1977 [[Sigrun Schleipfer]] founded the '[Gemeinschaft zur Erhaltung der Burgen](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gemeinschaft_zur_Erhaltung_der_Burgen&action=edit&redlink=1 "Gemeinschaft zur Erhaltung der Burgen (page does not exist)")' (Society for the Conservation of Castles), which proclaims castles to be among the _"last paradises of the romantic era"_ in this cold modern age and had as its primary aim the purchase and restoration of a castle for the Order. In Yule 1995, the society finally acquired the castle of [Rothenhorn](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Rothenhorn&action=edit&redlink=1 "Rothenhorn (page does not exist)") in [Szlichtyngowa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Szlichtyngowa "Szlichtyngowa") in [Poland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poland "Poland"), a run-down structure dating back to the 12th century, though most of the complex dates from the 16th century.
