(March 21, 1871 - September 21, 1961) was an American [astrologer](https://hatch.kookscience.com/wiki/Category:Astrology "Category:Astrology") and follower of [George W. Carey](https://hatch.kookscience.com/wiki/George_W._Carey "George W. Carey"), with whom she co-authored _God-Man: The Word Made Flesh_ (1920), and upon whose body of work she promoted _Biochemistry_ through her Carey-Perry School of the Chemistry of Life.
