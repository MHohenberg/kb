The heir of the FRA and the GKK of the OTO in Austria and Switzerland was undoubtedly Hermann Joseph Metzger - Tau Josephus, Master Paragranus (b.1919-f.1990); who was ordained Bishop by [[Herbert Fritsche|Fritsche]] in October 1957, in Stuttgart, with his disciple [[Anita Borgert]] as witness. Subsequently, in 1960, seven weeks before his death, Fritsche "in an unstable state of mental health" cedes his position as Patriarch of the GKK to Metzger (Kónig, 1994). After this appointment he moves the Summun Supremun Sanctuarium to Thelema Abbey in Stein, Switzerland (González, 1968). After the death of K. Germer, successor of Crowley's [[O.T.O|OTO]] in 1962, Paragranus was denied recognition as the great general head of the OTO, as the alleged documents were not convincing. Consequently, Paragranus founded his own organization "Ordo Illuminatorum" (Ol) to be part of the OTO as a revived "Illuminati Order" (König, 1994).

Paragranus made several attempts to unite all branches of the [[Fraternitas Rosicruciana Antiqua|FRA]] under his own jurisdiction. Among his strategies he proceeded to make an advertisement in 1963, in the famous “[[Astrological Yearbook of Elias Bucheli]]” (who was a disciple of [[Master Huiracocoha|Master Huiracocoha]]) where he invited all Latin American members of the FRA to meet in Stein (Bucheli, 1963). [[Ana Delia González]], Commander of the FRA in Venezuela, responded to the invitation. On that visit she received the title of "Consul for the countries of Latin America and Spain", whose original certificate is preserved in the Archives of the Aula Madre Luz y Razón in Maracaibo. In December 1963 he sent notifications to all the FRAs requesting them to recognize his authority. The Aulas of Argentina, Chile, Bolivia, Peru, Nicaragua, Guatemala, Santo Domingo and Spain continued their membership in the Council of Maestra Yedosey (González, 1964).
## Prozess gegen Horst Knaut
A.- Am 25. März 1972 erschien in Nr. 13 der Zeitschrift "Neue Illustrierte Revue", Heinrich Bauer Verlag, Hamburg auf den Seiten 86-89 ein bebilderter Artikel unter dem Titel "Die Botschaft aus der Geisterwelt", der zur Hauptsache über angebliche Vorgänge in der "[[Abtei Thelema]]" in Stein/AR berichtete.

B.- Hermann Metzger, Schriftsteller, Zürich, und [[Anita Borgert]], Geschäftsführerin, Stein/AR, die im erwähnten Artikel mit Namen genannt worden waren, klagten gegen den als verantwortlich zeichnenden Redaktor [[Dr. Walter Kunze]], Stuttgart, und den Journalisten [[Horst Knaut ]](Schwarzenbach/BRD) wegen Ehrverletzung.

Die Verteidigung erhob gegen die Klage die Einrede der örtlichen Unzuständigkeit, weil die Tat im Ausland begangen worden sei. Ferner machte sie geltend, infolge des Rückzugs der Klage gegen Dr. Kunze fehle ein gültiger Strafantrag.

Mit Vorfrageentscheid vom 11. September 1975 hat das Kantonsgericht von Appenzell A.Rh., 1. Abteilung, beide Prozesseinreden abgewiesen.
