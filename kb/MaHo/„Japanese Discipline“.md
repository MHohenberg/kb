1. Shugyo - Ritual suffering - The idea that repeating discomfort will eventually make the body accept it as ‘base level’, thus making you stronger. 
2. Ikigai - „Your reason for being“ - Necessary to make suffering have meaning. „If people have reason to get up every morning, they live longer“.
3. Kodawari - Never settling for less than excellence in every detail - 
4. Shikata ga nai - It can not be helped - emotional clarity about what is within and outside of your area of control.
5. Kintsugi - ‘golden joining’ - originally about pottery repair, it means that breaks and repairs are part of an objects story and not bad marks against it. 
6. Kaizen - continuous small improvements > dramatic changes. 
7. danshari - mess than breaks focus - Reduce possession to create mental clarity.
