## Motivation
What does my character want?
What are their goals and aspirations?
What motivates them to pursue these goals?
What obstacles would stand in their way?
How do they react to setbacks and challenges?
What is their driving force in their decisions/actions?

## Beliefs and values
What moral code does my character follow?
What are their values and principles
What do they stand for?
What do they oppose?

## Results come from action: Playing the part
Character interview
- Write down actual interview questions for your alter ego, then answer the questions as your character.
	- Can you tell me a bit about your background and how it has influenced who you are today
	- Tell me about your biggest strengths and weaknesses
	- How do you typically react to difficult or challenging situations?
	- What motivates you and keeps you going when you face obstacles or setbacks?
	- Can you describe your current values and beliefs for me? How do they guide your decisions / actions?
	- How do you typically interact with others? What kind of communication styles do you like?
