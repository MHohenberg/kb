Zunächst geheimes Forschungsprogramm der [[CIA]]. Forschungsziel war die Nutzung von Drogen bei Verhören. Offiziell zwischen 1953-1970

In Unterprogramm 8 wurde die Funktion von [[LSD]] erforscht. 

MKOFTEN
It was the most diverse of the programs, involving mind altering substances, incapacitating drugs, and black magic. Dr. Gottlieb employed a diverse cast of occult schools in the project: astrologers, mystics, mediums, psychics, voodoo practitioners, sorcerers, witches, warlocks and satanists. MK OFTEN is the program responsible for the creation of heart attack serum, and involved the testing of these lethal and mind warping drugs on captive inmates.
